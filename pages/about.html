Our Journey & Mission

Stackly was founded on a simple premise: early-stage tech companies spend too much time building internal metrics and infrastructure, instead of focusing on their core products. We built Stackly to serve as the unified operational engine for high-growth tech startups.

2023

Founding Vision

Started in New York City by a team of startup engineers to build the ultimate telemetry and analytics dashboard dashboard tool.

2024

Seed Expansion

Raised $4.5M in seed funding to expand the engineering group and launch the real-time collaboration workspace environment.

2025

Global Integration

Introduced multi-cloud pipeline features, enabling seamless database synchronization and automated alerts across all major messaging tools.

Empowering Tech Innovation

We are committed to helping startups scale. From data visualization to secure user permissioning, Stackly takes care of the heavy lifting.
